# Outline

## Structure
Multi-POV science fiction. Each early chapter covers the same event — the intake/implantation process — from a different human's perspective. Same horror, different mind.

## Themes
- Primates as vermin: the full inversion of human exceptionalism
- Misunderstood intelligence: the squids are wrong about humans, coherently
- Ethical ambiguity without resolution: 0.3̅ is sympathetic and monstrous simultaneously
- The dystopia that doesn't know it's a dystopia

## Act 1: Intake

### Ch 1: "Raj" (POV: Raj, ~2500 words)
- BEATS:
  1. Wakes in tube, confronts 0.3̅ directly
  2. Jellyfish implantation — body terror, paralysis
  3. Chitinous nubs form at temples
  4. First fractured telepathic contact — familiar voices, wrong content
  5. Squids argue (bluish vs green) — Raj catches fragments, realizes he's the subject
  6. 0.3̅ returns: "Let's get you some tentacles. You're going to be my little helper."
- PLANTS:
  - Raj's neural profile is unusual — 0.3̅ chose him specifically (payoff: later)
  - Xi Pei already being interrogated about iPhone (payoff: Ch 3)
- EMOTIONAL ARC: disoriented rationalization → violation → forced lucidity
- STATUS: drafted (v1, score 8.0)

### Ch 2: "Irina" (POV: Irina, ~2500 words)
- BEATS:
  1. Wakes from fractal dream — tentacle already at her face
  2. Attempts to scream — signal never connects, panic detonates internally
  3. Implantation — she tries to classify and locate every sensation as it happens
  4. Blackout, resurfaces to two squids arguing in bioluminescent bursts
  5. First telepathic contact — her brother's voice, telling her to be quiet
  6. She realizes: the squids are arguing about the humans. She is cargo.
  7. 0.3̅ returns to her tube. Irina watches her work with analytical horror.
- PLANTS:
  - Irina's instinct to reverse-engineer everything — she's already studying the implant (payoff: later)
  - Her brother's voice as the translation artifact — something in her emotional architecture (payoff: later)
- EMOTIONAL ARC: rage → forced stillness → cold analytical focus
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 3: "Xi Pei" (POV: Xi Pei, ~2000 words)
- BEATS:
  1. Xi Pei is already past intake — implant settled, nubs formed
  2. Watches 0.3̅ interrogate other humans about the iPhone — they all fail
