### Chapter 18

Xi Pei planted his feet on the bridge deck. Slick residue glued his toes, flux-thick under suit membrane.

0.3̅ dominated center space. Mantle rippled teal gradients. One eye fixed forward, others sealed. Delta Del coiled left, orange-red flares stabbing dim.

Fractal readouts twisted walls. She extended channel. Logistics vector, clean.

Nubs itched at his temples. He keyed acceptance. Signal passed undistorted.

Her display quickened. Pulses layered deadline pinch. Delta Del countered, jagged crimson shredding water. Shenzhen rasp slammed his nubs: *Suboptimal. Fried three. Cull line now.*

Heart thudded against ribs. Jaw locked. Tongue swelled salt-bitter.

Nubs fired network-wide. "Slab design. Human architecture bridge."

0.3̅'s bioluminescence cracked white-hot. Eye swiveled. Locked.

Grandmother voice probed through nubs. "Information."

"Back on the line, he'd run six stations. Knew yield rates by part number. Knew why the slab worked: layered tolerances, silicon doped precise, electrons forced through gates by voltage differentials."

She processed. Gradients reset teal. "Terms."

"Six weeks extension. Post-delivery: others preserved. Pods intact. No further implants. No suit growth. Isolation from fields. Viable units."

Delta Del recoiled, body swelling eruption-brink as flares peaked crimson-black, tentacles lashing probe-wires loose while telepathic jabs overloaded Xi Pei's nubs like defect scanners rejecting scrap—*Rip them now, slurry the line, your meat first into vats*—water churning deck in sync with his hammering pulse, fractals redlining deadlines, orange bursts boring hunger's calculus into the bridge as grandmother-voice threaded caution through cost matrices spinning wild across 0.3̅'s skin, Delta Del's spikes demanding immediate disassembly of the entire meat-line into yields his channels could already parse as optimal cull.

Jaw ground. Nubs held tolerances. "Bureaucracy attunement. Inputs set. Outputs clean. Yield exceeds thresholds."

Delta Del's flares guttered. Bark jagged through overload. "Disposal."

"No," he keyed back, nubs steady. "Eat bridge meat, lose parse."

0.3̅'s eye narrowed. Teal stabilized. *Extension granted. Lattice access provisional.*

Delta Del's mantle spasmed—private spike to her: *Audit flags your pets. Land-dwellers circle.* Betrayal calculus, hidden from Xi Pei.

"We consider sealed," 0.3̅ said.

Bridge irised shut. Nubs hummed blank.

His attunement held tolerances.
