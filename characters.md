Others are fried. 0.3̅ tracks the survival rate with scientific interest.
- **"Let's get you some tentacles."** She means this as a compliment. An elevation.
- **Relationship to humans:** Keeps them in her lab in suspended animation pods. Brings them out to terrariums. Studies them. Grows attached in ways she can't fully justify by her society's framework.

## UNIVERSE NOTES (for chapter writer context)

### Cephalopod Society
- Primates = vermin tier. Mouse-equivalent. Unclean. Befoul their environment. Cannot coexist with them.
- Humans are black market goods: pets (rare, taboo, illegal) or food (common, unremarkable).
- Earth = protected galactic nature preserve. Contact forbidden. This crew is operating illegally.
- Castes: Aquatic (highest — intelligent, cultured), Amphibious (land operations), Land-dwelling (marginalized, considered disabled).

### Communication
- Cephalopod communication is **both bioluminescent and telepathic** — simultaneous, inseparable channels.
- Also grounded in a math-based language (geometry, calculus, trigonometry). Squids think in math.
- Humans perceive cephalopod communication as synesthesia — color, sound, taste, memory, emotion arriving at once. Overwhelming before the implant. Partially decipherable after.
- Individual squids look nearly identical to unimplanted humans. Implanted humans begin to distinguish them.

### Bio-Technology
- All cephalopod tech is **biological — grown, not built.** Ships, tools, suits, implants. Nothing mechanical.
- Tools appear to humans as colorful fractal jellyfish or amoebas that melt onto and into surfaces.
- **Jellyfish implants:** Applied to a human host, they begin terraforming the nervous system. Chitinous nubs grow from the temples — the physical interface for telepathic communication. Some hosts sync. Some are fried. 0.3̅ tracks outcomes.
- **Squid suits:** Implant layers accumulate over time into a full bio-suit. Enables aquatic survival. Grows basic bioluminescent capability — enough for rudimentary squid communication.
- The conversion process is not described as cruelty by the squids. It is described as improvement.

### Cephalopod Appearance (human POV)
- 30–40 feet tall. Three or more eyes set around the body.
- When addressing humans, squids close their extra eyes — leaving one open. Deliberate. A power display that also makes communication possible.
- Bioluminescent displays are incomprehensibly fast to unimplanted humans. Post-implant, humans begin to catch fragments.
- To unimplanted humans: terrifying giants. To implanted humans: terrifying giants who are starting to make sense.
